Agile Mindset

Agile is not a principal or a method, but it’s an integral part of being Agile that is guided by principles, defined by values and manifested through various practices. At Karmisheba Technology, we firmly believe in cultivating Agile mindset to adopt the change and learn through continual improvement to accelerate delivery and deliver amazing results.

01 Respect

Respect for the culture, as well as a human, is the first and foremost human need. When you treat our colleagues with respect, then without a doubt they are empowered to improve their practices. The principle of respect between the teammates also extends to the relationships with partners, suppliers, customers and the wider community that supports the enterprise.

02 Coordination and Collaboration

Have you ever wondered why Agile demands cross-functional team meetings to conduct the session together? If you put coders, testers, and other experts together, then their inputs create value for the whole project, and it promotes knowledge between different teams and facilitates an Agile though process.

03 Improvement Cycle

'You'll never reach perfection because there's always room for improvement. And we believe in supporting such behavior and embrace procedural adherence.

04 Failures’ as Opportunities to Grow

Agile Helps in Successful Project Delivery: Whitepaper

Today’s world is an uncertain one and it is difficult to predict what will happen in the future. Such unforeseen events and organizational changes can have a negative impact on project delivery. Agile project management provides an approach that offers agility while retaining project concepts, project delivery, and project management. With agile project management, the organization gains the benefits of the agile approach without unnecessary risks.

The modern world is globalized, customer-focused, responsive and driven by technology. At the center of it all is software.
